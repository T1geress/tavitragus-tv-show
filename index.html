The Dragon Prince

Overview

The Dragon Prince follows a group of heroes as they try to save the world from approaching war. It starts with a gang of elfin assasins who have come to get revenge on the King of Katolis. They are spotted and the youngest is sent to kill the one who spotted them, but she cannot bring herself to do it. Then, she meets the princes, sees that the Dragon Prince actually isn't destroyed. She and the princes then go on a long adventure to right the world and return the dragon prince and fight off evil from detroying magic in the world.

Seasons

The Dragon Prince currently has 3 seasons, and a fourth is coming out sometime in the future. In the first season, the dragon prince hatches. In the second season, the travelors travel across the remaining part of the human kingdoms, but Ezran finds out he is now king, and goes back to be the King. The other 2 continue their journey an cross into Zadia. In the thrid season, they fight a dragon at the entrance to Zadia, continue through Zadia, have a big battle, kill the bad guy, and have the bad guy brought back to life.
