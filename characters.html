The Dragon Prince

Ezran

Ezran is the prince of Katolis, son of King Harrow of Katolis. He has the special ability of speaking and understand animals. Ezran also has a pet glowtoad named Bait. He, his brother, and Reyla go an an adventure to return Azymondious, a baby dragon, to his mother. He also loves stealing jelly tarts from the castle baker.

Callum

Callum is the adopted prince of Katolis, son of King Harrow of Katolis. He is the only human that can perform natural magic and is drawn to sky magic. He also loves drawing. He, his brother, and Reyla go an an adventure to return Azymondious, a baby dragon, to his mother. Callum also loves drawing.

Rayla

Rayla is an elf who was orgionally sent to assassinate Prince Ezran and King Harrow of Katolis. Then, she befriends the Princes and they go on a massive adventure to save the world and return the Dragon Prince to his rightful place in Zadia. Rayla is smart, agile, and friendly.

Azymondias (Zym)

Zym is a storm dragon, the most powerful type, and will grow up to be the king of all dragons. He hatches at the end of the first season and continues with the adventurers on to Zadia, the land of magic, to take his throne. Zym is loveable, powerful, and loving.

Soren

Soren is the son of the main bad guy, Lord Viren. He is very "masculine" as in very club you first, ask you questions later. He has several internal conflicts because he is loyal to his king but also loyal to his father, and they have conflicts of interest. He does some bad things, but also tries to make up.

Claudia

Claudia is the daughtor of the main bad guy, Lord Viren. She is also a very powerful dark mage and loyal to her father. At the end of the show that is currently available, she does a potentially awful deed to bring him back.
